About Us

Ethars was an idea for a Peer-to-Peer, cross-platform, instant messaging system. We wanted to create a messaging platform that allowed seamless connection between everyone without any hassle.

Why Are We Different?

As said before, Ethars is a Peer-to-Peer instant messaging system, and it is also End-to-End encrypted. This means that we cannot spy on your messages or steal and use your information.
